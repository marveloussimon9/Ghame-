✦ Cycle Wisdom

Live in sync with your rhythm

Your cycle is a superpower. These daily practices help you work with your body, not against it.

01

Honor Your Rest Phase

During menstruation, slow down with restorative yoga, warming foods, and extra sleep. Your body is doing hard work.

02

Harness Follicular Energy

Rising estrogen boosts creativity and motivation. This is the perfect time for new projects and social connection.

03

Peak Power at Ovulation

Your communication and confidence peak. Schedule important meetings, workouts, and social events now.

04

Ground in the Luteal Phase

Progesterone rises — use this introspective time for deep work, journaling, and nourishing comfort foods.
